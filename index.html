Getting Started

🔹 Use Unity (preferably 2020 or later) with XR Interaction Toolkit.

🔹 Import the Photon PUN 2 package for multiplayer networking.

🔹 Optimize physics settings for better performance.

Optimization Tips

🔹 Use low-poly models and baked lighting for better performance.

🔹 Reduce unnecessary physics calculations (e.g., limit colliders).

🔹 Implement LOD (Level of Detail) for maps with large environments.

Multiplayer Setup

🔹 Use Photon PUN 2 or Fish-Networking for smooth multiplayer.

🔹 Sync player movement efficiently to reduce lag.

🔹 Test with different connection speeds to optimize networking.

PlayFab Integration

🔹 Use PlayFab for account management and inventory.

🔹 Store player cosmetics and stats in PlayFab Cloud.

🔹 Sync wearable items (like staff sweaters) across devices.
